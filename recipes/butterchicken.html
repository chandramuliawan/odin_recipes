BUTTER CHICKEN

serves 4, maybe 2 if we are very hungry

butter chicken recipe that i learn during my stay in 'Straya


the end result probably won't look like this

Ingredients

aka what you will need
  • 1kgs of juicy chicken thighs. chicken breast is okay too!
  • half cup of plain yogurt
  • Onion, diced. adjust amount as you like
  • Garlic, minced. adjust amount as you like
  • BUTTER not a butter chicken if not with these, with amount adjusted to your liking!
  • 100gr of crushed tomatoes
  • and some south asian spices! a wee bit too lazy to list, but hey this is HTML building practice! not cooking!

Steps by steps

aka how to cook 'em
  1. Diced the chicken in, um, bit sized pieces as preferred
  2. Use the minced garlic, and some crushed ginger if you like, and add the yogurt and the seasonings into the chicked. Rub them good, and let is sit for 4 hours at the very least. left it overnight sounds cool too! let them sleep tight in the fridge yo!
  3. After the chicken is well rested, we can start cook the thing! Start by melting your butter!
  4. After the butter is hot and ready, add the onions. hint: if you have more garlic, we can add them along with the onion as well!
  5. Soon as the onions are golden and caramelised, add the chicken into the pot!
  6. Make sure the chicken is well cooked in all direction and side! You are welcome to cooked the chicken beforehand and/or in separate pot, if you like.
  7. After all the chicken is juiced up, add the crushed tomatoes. This crushed tomatoes will act as the sauce of the dish. So you can add more or add less to your liking!
  8. Since all the ingredients are all cooked, all that's left is to left the sauce to simmer. Just stop when we arrived at the consistency that suites your liking!

Some Pro tip!

  • Feel free to add some veggies to make or to make it looks like more healthy!
  • Add some thick cream to help adjust consistency as well as the colour of the dish! in case you find the colour is too red, maybe
